Make meme and reaction GIFs from long videos

Grab a reaction moment from a long clip, tighten it into a clean loop, and export a GIF for fast replies on social media or community channels.

Reaction GIF process

  1. Upload your long clip or highlight segment.
  2. Trim to the exact reaction and remove dead time before/after.
  3. Adjust playback speed to make the loop punchier.
  4. Add optional caption text and export as GIF.

What to use this for

  • Community manager reaction replies on X and Reddit.
  • Meme format posts for launch-day engagement.
  • Quick visual replies in Slack and Discord threads.

FAQ

Can I create text-caption memes too? Yes, add overlays before exporting.

Do I need editing software installed? No, trimmr runs fully in your browser.
