Create video memes in your browser

Use trimmr to turn short clips into captioned video memes. Trim the exact moment, add top and bottom text, tweak speed, and export — all without uploading your media.

How to make a video meme

  1. Open trimmr and select your MP4 or WebM clip.
  2. Trim the clip so the setup and punchline feel tight.
  3. Add a text overlay for the top caption, then duplicate or add another for the bottom.
  4. Adjust playback speed if the reaction feels too slow or fast.
  5. Export as MP4, WebM, or GIF depending on where you plan to share it.

Ideas for video memes

  • Reaction memes from movies, TV episodes, or livestreams.
  • Work or coding jokes from screen recordings or call clips.
  • Before/after reveals with top text for the setup and bottom text for the payoff.

Why use trimmr for memes?

  • Your clips stay on your device — trimming and export run in the browser.
  • Fast loop for testing different captions and speeds.
  • One editor for both GIF-style loops and MP4/WebM exports.

FAQ

Can I add multiple text boxes? Yes, you can add multiple overlays for top and bottom captions.

Which formats work best for sharing? MP4 and WebM are great for social feeds; GIF works well for chat and forums.

Does my video upload to a server? No, trimmr processes media locally in your browser.
