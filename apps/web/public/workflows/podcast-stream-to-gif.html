Turn podcast and stream moments into GIFs

Pull highlight moments from long episodes, interviews, or livestreams and turn them into quick GIFs for social posts, community replies, and recap threads.

Fast clip-to-GIF workflow

  1. Choose a stream or podcast clip from your device.
  2. Trim down to the exact highlight or reaction.
  3. Optionally speed up or slow down the loop.
  4. Export as GIF for posting to social channels.

When this is useful

  • Pull quotable moments from long interview episodes.
  • Share streamer reactions as quick community replies.
  • Build weekly highlight GIF threads for audience growth.

FAQ

Does trimmr upload my source clip? No. Processing stays local in your browser.

Can I also export MP4? Yes, switch export format when you need a video version.
