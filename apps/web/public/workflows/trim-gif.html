Trim GIF online free (private, no upload)

trimmr is a fast video trimmer that runs in your browser. Your file stays on your device, so you can cut loops quickly without uploading media to a server.

How to trim a GIF

  1. Open trimmr and choose your GIF.
  2. Drag trim start and trim end to keep only the moment you want.
  3. Preview, optionally add text, then export your result.

Why creators use this page

  • Remove dead frames before and after a reaction loop.
  • Make social GIFs shorter for better attention and file size.
  • Keep private media local while editing.

FAQ

Do I need an account? No, trimmr is free with no sign-up.

Can I export to other formats? Yes. You can export GIF, WebM, MP4, and animated WebP.

Does my file upload? No. Processing happens in your browser.
