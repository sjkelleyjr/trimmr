How to add text to a GIF

  1. Choose your GIF (or video, then convert).
  2. Click Add caption, type text, then position it.
  3. Adjust font and background styling, then export.

Ideal use cases

  • Meme captions and reaction text overlays.
  • Short subtitle-like context for silent loops.
  • Branded CTA text on promotional snippets.

FAQ

Can I move the text anywhere? Yes, captions are draggable on the preview.

Can I export non-GIF formats? Yes, export to GIF, WebM, MP4, or animated WebP.
